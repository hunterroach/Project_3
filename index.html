About Us

Founded: 2005
Founder: Hunter Roach

Overview

Roach's Rides was founded in 2005 by Hunter Roach with the vision of creating a seamless, eco-friendly, and accessible public transportation system. Initially, the company focused on connecting underserved urban areas through affordable buses and shuttles. Over time, Roach's Rides evolved into a leading innovator in sustainable urban transit, offering cutting-edge technology and efficient solutions to reduce environmental impact.

Roach's Rides is now a cornerstone of urban mobility, providing clean energy transit options and advocating for smarter transportation habits. The company strives to empower communities by making public transport a cleaner, more viable alternative to private vehicles.
