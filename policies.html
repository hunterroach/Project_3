Financing Policies

At Roach's Rides, we offer flexible financing options to make our products and services accessible to everyone. Our financing policies include:

  • 0% interest financing available for qualified customers for up to 12 months.
  • Flexible payment plans tailored to meet your budget.
  • Financing options are subject to approval through our partnered financial institutions.
  • No early repayment penalties for loans.

If you have any questions about financing, please contact our support team for assistance.

Delivery Policies

We ensure safe and timely delivery of all our products. Our delivery policies include:

  • Free delivery for purchases within a 50-mile radius.
  • For locations beyond 50 miles, a delivery fee is calculated based on distance.
  • All deliveries are scheduled within 2-5 business days of purchase confirmation.
  • Customers will receive a tracking link to monitor the delivery status.
  • Special handling and assembly services are available upon request.

For more information about delivery schedules and fees, contact us directly.

Return Policies

Your satisfaction is our priority. Our return policies are designed to be fair and simple:

  • Products can be returned within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt.
  • Items must be in their original condition, with all components and packaging intact.
  • Custom or special-order items are not eligible for returns.
  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days after the return is received and inspected.
  • Return shipping fees apply unless the product is defective or incorrect.

If you need to initiate a return, please contact our customer service team for further instructions.
